Margot slade Attempts poetry

up in a tree a squirrel senses a bee, a dog walks with zest, my day has just begun. though my head is hung, I am full of wonder, I think of all that I see. and throughout my hardships i try my best, with agile feet, the ring of thunder extemporizing the journey as i walk, i paint with swift movements. Art means I musnt always talk, unlike poetry.
